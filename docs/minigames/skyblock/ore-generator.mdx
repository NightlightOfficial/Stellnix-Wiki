# Ore Generator
#### Your gateway to ores and cobblestone
Unlike the classic SkyBlock, our version of the "Cobblestone generator" also generates ores!
Depending on the pickaxe you're holding while mining, different ores have the chance to be generated.

### How to build it
Bulding the Ore Generator is simple, all you need for it is the lava and water buckets you get in the starter chest!

## Nether Ores
Wanna go to the Nether? You can have a taste of it by crafting the **Obsidian Pickaxe**.


![Obsidian Pickaxe Crafting](/wiki-files/obsidian-pickaxe-crafting.png)

You will need **3 Obsidian** and **2 sticks** to craft it. You can find Obsidian in chests on various [basic islands](/docs/minigames/skyblock/islands#basic-islands)!

Using the Obsidian Pickaxe on the Ore Generator will cause it to generate **only** the following Nether blocks and ores:
- Netherrack
- Obsidian
- Quartz
- Nether Gold Ore
- Ancient Debris
