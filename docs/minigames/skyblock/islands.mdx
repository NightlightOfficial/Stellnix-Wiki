# Islands
#### Access various resources and mobs on other islands!

Each island on Stellnix SkyBlock has different blocks, items, biome and mobs. 
On all islands are also chests with items, in some of which, you can find Obsidian, necessary to craft the [Obsidian Pickaxe](/docs/minigames/skyblock/ore-generator#nether-ores).

## Basic Islands
During the day, these islands aren't dangerous.
Their chests also contain Obsian blocks and are a necessary stepping stone to unlock more blocks and items.

### Starter
Everyone starts here, your homebase for all things!
Nothing too special about this island at a first glance, but it has a lot!
There's **dirt**, **gravel** and **oak trees**

![Starter Island](/wiki-files/starter-island.png)

### Sakura
Friend-shaped island with the perfect biome for mob spawning!
Be prepared to put a fence around this guy, because sheep, cows, chicken and more love to spawn here!

You can find **dirt**, **cherry blossom trees** and a **beehive** there

:::note
There's also a **Villager**! Make sure to protect them at all cost, because once they die, there's no going back!
:::

![Sakura Island](/wiki-files/sakura-island.png)

### Sahara
Very hot island, but has water so our dear friend there doesn't die of thirst.

You can find **sand**, **sandstone**, **cacti**, **sugar canes**, and a **water source** there

:::note
There's also a **Villager**! Make sure to protect them at all cost, because once they die, there's no going back!
:::


![Sahara Island](/wiki-files/sahara-island.png)

### Creaky
Gives *me* the creeps.. get it? Jokes aside, this island is harmless during the day

You can find **dirt**, **pale oak trees** and -- todo -- there

![Creaky Island](/wiki-files/creaky-island.png)

## Advanced Islands
So you want to get more? Be careful, these islands can be dangerous, so suit up!

### Nether
Wanna get brewing? This island contains a **Blaze** spawner.

![Nether Island](/wiki-files/nether-island.png)

### Guardian
Honestly, this island should have its own category, because it is a **boss island**.
On this island is a chest, heavily guarded, but the reward is worth it, trust me.
