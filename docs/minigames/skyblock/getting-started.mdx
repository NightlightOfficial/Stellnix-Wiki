# Stellnix SkyBlock
#### Survive alone or with friends on a floating island! Stellnix-style!

## Getting Started
If you've played SkyBlock before, you can skip this section and move to Islands

You spawn on a small grass island with 1 chest. This chest includes some items, including the key items:
- **Water bucket**   
- **Lava Bucket**

Do NOT lose those items, as they are necessary to play SkyBlock

:::note

If something goes wrong on your island, you can reset it by using /sb reset once a day.

:::
